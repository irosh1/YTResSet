YTResSet - User Consent

Welcome to YTResSet, a Firefox extension that enhances your YouTube viewing experience!

What YTResSet Does:

  • Allows you to set custom video resolutions for specific YouTube channels
  • Automatically applies your preferred quality settings when watching videos
  • Stores your preferences locally on your device

Privacy and Data Usage:

  • YTResSet does not collect or transmit any personal data
  • Your channel preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We do not track your browsing history or video watching habits
