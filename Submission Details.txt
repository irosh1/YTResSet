Summary:
YTResSet enhances your YouTube experience by allowing you to set custom video resolutions for specific channels. It automatically applies your preferred quality settings, ensuring optimal viewing across different YouTube channels.


Description:
<p>YTResSet is a powerful Firefox add-on designed to enhance your YouTube viewing experience. With YTResSet, you have full control over video quality settings for each YouTube channel you watch.</p>

<h3>Key Features:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Set custom video resolutions for specific YouTube channels</li>
  <li>Automatically apply your preferred quality settings when watching videos</li>
  <li>Easily manage and update your channel-specific preferences</li>
  <li>Seamless integration with YouTube's interface</li>
  <li>Lightweight and efficient performance</li>
</ul>

<h3>How It Works:</h3>
<p>Once installed, YTResSet allows you to specify your preferred video quality for each YouTube channel. When you visit a video on that channel, the add-on automatically sets the video to your preferred resolution. This saves you the hassle of manually adjusting quality settings for each video you watch.</p>

<h3>Privacy and Data Usage:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>YTResSet does not collect or transmit any personal data</li>
  <li>Your channel preferences are stored locally on your device</li>
  <li>We do not track your browsing history or video watching habits</li>
</ul>
